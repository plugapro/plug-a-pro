## THE DAILY DISPATCH CHECKLIST
**Run this every morning before 8am**

### ✅ Jobs Confirmed
- [ ] All jobs for today confirmed with customers (no "are you still coming?" calls)
- [ ] All customers have technician name and arrival window
- [ ] Cancellations/reschedules from yesterday reassigned

### ✅ Technicians Briefed
- [ ] Each technician knows their first job address and arrival time
- [ ] Each technician has the customer's WhatsApp number
- [ ] No technician has more than [X] jobs without buffer time

### ✅ Equipment and Parts
- [ ] Parts ordered yesterday confirmed for delivery or pickup
- [ ] Each technician has correct tools for today's jobs
- [ ] Common consumable stock checked

### ✅ Open Quotes
- [ ] Quotes sent 2+ days ago without a reply — followed up
- [ ] Quotes accepted yesterday — converted to bookings

### ✅ Outstanding Payments
- [ ] Invoices older than 7 days — payment reminder sent
- [ ] Jobs completed yesterday without payment — flagged

### ✅ Yesterday's Exceptions
- [ ] Customer complaints from yesterday acknowledged and assigned
- [ ] Jobs that couldn't be completed rescheduled
