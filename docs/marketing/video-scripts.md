# Short-Form Video Scripts — Plug A Pro

> Platform: Instagram Reels, Facebook Video
> Priority: Start with Script 1 and 2

---

## Script 1 — "The WhatsApp Problem" (30s)

**Hook (0–3s):** "This is how most service businesses in South Africa manage 8 technicians."
**Show (3–12s):** Chaotic WhatsApp group — voice notes, unread messages, unanswered customers
**Narration:** "Customer requests come in on personal numbers. Jobs assigned in group chats. Quotes forgotten. Payments chased weeks later."
**Pivot (12–20s):** "This is what it looks like on Plug A Pro."
**Show:** Clean dashboard — job in, assigned, customer updated, invoice sent
**Close (28–30s):** "Same WhatsApp. Better system. Link in bio."

---

## Script 2 — "Day in the Life" (45s)

**Hook:** "A typical Tuesday for a 6-technician plumbing business — before and after Plug A Pro."
**Before (3–20s):** 7am chaos → spreadsheet → customer complaint → invoice unsent
**After (20–40s):** Dashboard check → auto-dispatch → customer updated → invoice sent → payment received
**Close:** "Which Tuesday do you want? Link in bio."
