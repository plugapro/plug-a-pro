Plug A Pro — Matching · Shortlist · Acceptance · Credit Flow (v2)

Single-source state machine for the qualified-shortlist model. Shows JobRequest + LeadInvite states, the privacy boundary, and the exact point at which 1 credit is deducted. Credit is deducted exactly once — on the selected provider's final acceptance — and customer details unlock at the same instant in one atomic transaction.
