## How It Works — Customer Version

**Section label:** For customers  
**Section headline:** From job to done, in a few taps

**Steps:**

1. **Describe your job on WhatsApp**  
   Message the Plug A Pro number. Tell us what needs doing, where the job is, and when suits you. Add a photo if it helps. We'll create a proper job request from your description.

2. **We match you to a nearby local pro**  
   We find local service providers in your area with the right skills and availability. When a match accepts, we send you their profile and move things forward.

3. **Approve the quote in writing**  
   The local pro submits a written quote with the price and scope. You approve before any work starts. Nothing is agreed verbally.

4. **They arrive, do the job, and update you on WhatsApp**  
   Before and after photos are attached to the job record. You get WhatsApp updates as the job moves through each stage.

5. **Confirm completion and leave a review**  
   Once the job is done, you confirm the work. Leave a review to help other customers and reward good service. The whole job record stays on file if you ever need to refer back.

---

## How It Works — Service Provider Version

**Section label:** For service providers  
**Section headline:** Register once. Receive matched leads.

**Steps:**

1. **Register your skills and areas on WhatsApp**  
   Message Plug A Pro to start onboarding. Tell us your name, what types of work you do, which areas you cover, and your general availability. Upload any evidence or reference photos you have.

2. **Your application is reviewed**  
   Plug A Pro reviews your submission before activating your profile for marketplace matching. If approved, you receive a WhatsApp confirmation and can activate your provider profile.

3. **Receive lead previews on WhatsApp**  
   When a matching customer request comes in for your trade and area, you get a WhatsApp lead preview. See the job type, suburb, and timing before you decide whether to unlock it.

4. **Unlock and accept leads you want**  
   Unlock a lead with credits to see the full job details and customer contact information. Accept or decline — it's your choice. No obligation to take every lead.

5. **Do the job, quote, and get reviewed**  
   Submit a written quote. Update job status as you work. Upload before and after photos. Every completed job adds a real customer review to your profile, building the reputation that earns you more leads over time.

## Trust and Verification Section

**Section label:** Trust & safety  
**Section headline:** Clearer records. Less risk on both sides.

**Body:**
> Plug A Pro does not remove every risk — no platform can. But it does create a clearer paper trail than an informal offline deal: written quotes, before and after photos, staged contact sharing, and a job record you can point to if something goes wrong.

**Trust points:**

1. **Your number stays private**  
   Plug A Pro manages the intake, quote approval, and status updates from the platform number. You don't need to share your personal contact details just to get a job moving.

2. **Providers are eligibility-reviewed before receiving leads**  
   Every provider application is manually reviewed before marketplace access is granted. This is an eligibility check, not a guarantee of licensing, safety, or quality of work. What each provider has submitted — experience, skills, service areas, reference evidence — is visible on their profile.

3. **All quotes are in writing**  
   No work starts without a written, approved quote. The price and scope are on record. If extra work comes up on-site, it must be submitted and approved in writing before proceeding.

4. **Before and after photos on every job**  
   Providers attach photos at the start and end of every job. If there is a dispute about what happened, the visual record is already there.

5. **A job record you can refer to**  
   The quote, approval, status updates, photos, and completion history stay on file. If anything needs to be reviewed — by support, or just by you — it is all in one place.

6. **Dispute support through Plug A Pro**  
   If something goes wrong, contact Plug A Pro support. We review the written record and help mediate. We do not guarantee financial resolution for every situation, but we do take each dispute seriously and follow a consistent review process.

**Important note:**
> Providers on Plug A Pro are independent service providers, not Plug A Pro employees. Plug A Pro facilitates the match and provides a structured, documented process for both sides. The quality and legality of the work remains the responsibility of the provider. See our Terms and Trust & Safety page for detail.
